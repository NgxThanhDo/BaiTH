Michigan Facts

Introduction to Michigan

Michigan is a state located in the Great Lakes region of the United States. It is well known for its natural beauty, automotive industry, and freshwater coastline.

Interesting Facts

  • Michigan has the longest freshwater coastline in the world.
  • Detroit is known as the birthplace of the American auto industry.
  • Michigan is surrounded by four of the five Great Lakes.
